Welcome to Vatsalya Sindhu

Keshava Seva Samithi is a Charitable Trust, started in 1999. At Vatsalya Sindhu,we have boys in the ages Arranging from 3 upwards-boys who are happy,energetic and aspire to achieve.our children are disciplane hand-working , chirpy, mischievous, child-like,empathetic and very affectionate above all they are young stars who seek opportunity like all children!.

Our special focus is on bringing a ray of hope into the lives of Slum dwelling families with particular stress on Children. We conduct classrooms for children under 13 and provide scholarship to those over 13, even upto Engineering and other graduation. We serve in fields of Education, Cultural nurturing, Health, and Women’s Empowerment areas. Our Organization is supported mostly through voluntary efforts with support from philanthropists & donors..
